## What This Skill Does

Checks the week's calendar, loads recipes and pantry inventory, proposes a 7-day meal plan, calculates a grocery list, adds the grocery trip to the calendar, and pushes the list to Google Keep.

1. Read `workflows/public/plan_meals.md` in full.
2. Follow the workflow from Step 1 through Step 8.
